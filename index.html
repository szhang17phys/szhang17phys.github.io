    Shuaixiang(Shu) Zhang

I am a PhD student (since fall of 2020) at Department of Physics of Indiana University, advised by Prof. Jon Urheim.

Currently I am involved in DUNE (Deep Underground Neutrino Exmperiment), which is hosted by Fermilab. Specifically, I work on the PDS (Photon Detector System) of protoDUNE-VD, located at CERN. I am mainly interested in analysis, simulation as well as hardware work. .

Prior to Indiana University, I graduated from Department of Modern Physics, University of Science and Technology of China [2016 - 2020].
